JAPAN MOONLIGHT FESTIVAL

Tsukimi, Mid-Autumn Festival in Japan Tsukimi, or Otsukimi is a traditional festival with a long history, considered as the Mid-Autumn Festival or Moon Festival in Japan. It falls on 15th day of 8th lunar month. The traditional customs on that day include appreciating the full moon, eating Tsukimi Dango, decorating house with pampas grass and flowers, etc... However, the customs of the festival are not kept well nowadays, and many young people do not celebrate this festival.
